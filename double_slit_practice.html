Key Formulas:
Constructive interference (maxima): d sin θ = mλ  (m = 0, ±1, ±2, ...)
Position on screen: y = L tan θ
Maximum order within angle θ: mmax = floor(d sin θ / λ)
Furthest maximum position: y = L · tan(arcsin(mmax · λ / d))
